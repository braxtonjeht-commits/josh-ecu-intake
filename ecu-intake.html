⚠️ Packing Requirements

✓ Wrap ECU in bubble wrap — at least 2 full layers
✓ Use a rigid box with padding on all sides (no jiffy bags)
✓ Include a printed copy of your order confirmation inside the box
✓ Secure all connectors — tape or bag any loose pins
✗ Do NOT ship in an envelope or padded mailer
✗ Do NOT send without the pre-paid label we email you
✗ ECUs damaged in transit due to improper packing cannot be serviced
